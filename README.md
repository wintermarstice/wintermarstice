<h3 align="center">Exploring the intersection between mathematics, systems philosophy, and code.</h3>

<br />

I strive to build meaningful systems by translating the pure logic of theoretical mathematics and physics into the deterministic world of code. For me, Artificial Intelligence is not *magic*; it's an engineering discipline that is both understandable and buildable upon first principles. My goal is not just to create systems that *work*, but to build elegant and robust systems where I know precisely *why and how they work*.

### 🧠 **My Philosophy**

* **Simplicity & Clarity:** I believe in breaking down complex problems into their simplest, most understandable fundamental parts.
* **Deep Understanding:** I'm not content with just using a tool; I strive to understand how it works, its philosophy, and its limitations. (That's why I've been a `Debian` user for 8 years.)
* **Logic over Obfuscation:** I stand against inefficiency, intrigue, and "office politics." I believe problems should be solved with logic and honest data.
* **Mastery > Competition:** My focus is not on beating others, but on understanding the essence and achieving mastery of a subject. Thus, my competition is none other than my past selves.
